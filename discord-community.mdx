Welcome to the Marmotte Discord Community! We've created a space where users, enthusiasts, contributors, and the Marmotte team can come together to discuss, share insights, and support one another.

## Why Join Our Discord Server?

### Real-time Support

Got a question or ran into an issue? Our community members and sometimes even the Marmotte development team are often active and ready to help.

### Latest Updates

Be the first to know about the latest features, updates, and announcements from the Marmotte team.

### Feedback and Suggestions

Share your feedback directly with us. Your insights are invaluable in shaping the future of Marmotte.

### Collaborate and Network

Connect with like-minded individuals, share knowledge, or even collaborate on exciting projects!

## How to Join

It's simple! Just click on the [permanent invite link](https://discord.gg/GHACsEaQ) and you'll be redirected to our Discord server. If you don't have a Discord account yet, you'll be prompted to create one.

### Server Rules

We believe in maintaining a respectful and productive environment. Here are some basic guidelines:

1. **Respect Everyone**: Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, bullying, or any form of discrimination will be tolerated.
2. **Stay on Topic**: Each channel has a specific topic. Please ensure your discussions are relevant.
3. **No Spam**: Refrain from posting unnecessary messages or self-promotion.
4. **Follow Discord's TOS**: All members must adhere to Discord's [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms).

Violation of these rules may result in a warning, mute, or ban from the server.

## Channels

Here's a brief overview of some of our primary channels:

- **#announcements**: Official updates and news from the Marmotte team.
- **#general-discussion**: A place for general chit-chat related to Marmotte.
- **#support**: Seek help for any issues or challenges you're facing.
- **#feature-requests**: Suggest new features or improvements.
- **#community-projects**: Share and discuss projects related to Marmotte.
